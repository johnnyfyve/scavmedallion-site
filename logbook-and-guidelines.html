You finally got the medal ... now what?

Medallion Protocol

  • Check in at the check-in page
  • Check the GPS and charge as needed
  • Plan and fly your own “Medallion” flight
  • Don’t keep it long and don’t hand it off to anyone who would
  • Log your flight in the Logbook; include art, documentation, drawings, and photos as desired
  • Deliver the Medallion, logbook, GPS, and both carrying cases to a new pilot
  • Contact “S.C.A.M. HQ” with problems, suggestions, and fresh ideas!
